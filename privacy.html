Privacy Policy

Last updated 17 June 2026.

Stoorie is a product of Submiti Ltd (company number 17205395), a company registered in England and Wales and registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO registration ZC162501). This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. If you have any question, email us at admin@stoorie.com.

The short version

Most of your private detail never leaves your phone. We never turn painful entries into career language. You can delete everything at any time, and we do not sell your data or show ads.

What we collect

Your account email, used to sign you in. The moments you write or speak. The shaped versions Stoorie creates from them (a story, a CV line, or a privately kept entry). Which experience areas a story relates to. And basic technical logs about engine speed and errors, which contain no story content.

How your private words are protected

Before anything is sent for shaping, Stoorie scans your text on your device and masks likely identifiers, such as names, emails, phone numbers and reference numbers, replacing them with neutral placeholders. Only the masked version is ever sent to our systems or to the AI engine. Your original, unmasked words and the record of what was masked are kept only on your device, not on our servers.

Who processes your data

We use Supabase to host the database and manage sign-in. We use Anthropic, the maker of the Claude AI model, to shape your masked text into usable language. Anthropic receives only the masked version, never your real names or identifiers. We do not sell your data, and we do not show advertising.

Entries you keep private

When an entry is about distress, grief or crisis rather than something you did, Stoorie keeps it as yours. It is never turned into career language, never given a CV line, and never used in any application, export or reminder. If you ever choose to shape one later, that is your decision and yours alone.

Why we are allowed to process it

We process your data to provide the service you have asked for, which is our agreement with you, and, where relevant, on the basis of your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

How long we keep it

We keep your data until you delete it. The "Delete all data" option in Settings permanently removes your entries from our database and clears the original words held on your device. Deleting cannot be undone.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you can ask to access, correct, export or erase your data, and you can object to or restrict how we process it. You can delete everything yourself in Settings, or contact us at admin@stoorie.com. You also have the right to complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

Security

Access to your data is limited to your own account, your private words are masked before they leave your device, and data is encrypted while in transit. No system is ever perfectly secure, but we take protecting your words seriously.

Children

Stoorie is intended for adults building a record of their working life. It is not directed at children under 16.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will change the date below, and for significant changes we will let you know in the app.
