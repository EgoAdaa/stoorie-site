Help

Questions we hear most. Still stuck? Email admin@stoorie.com.

What is Stoorie?

Stoorie helps you turn the real things you have done into language you can use in job applications and interviews. You write or speak what happened in your own words, and Stoorie shapes it into a clear story and a CV line, without inventing anything.

How is this different from using ChatGPT?

Stoorie keeps your record. It holds everything you capture over time and gives it back to you as evidence, and it is built never to invent facts. Your private details are also masked on your device before anything is sent, which a general chatbot does not do.

Does my data leave my phone? What about other people’s names?

Before anything is sent for shaping, Stoorie masks likely names, emails, phone numbers and reference numbers on your device. Only the masked version is sent. Your original words and the real names stay on your phone, not on our servers.

What happens to painful or private entries?

If an entry is about distress, grief or crisis rather than something you did, Stoorie keeps it as yours. It is never turned into career language, never given a CV line, and never used in an application or reminder. If you ever want to shape one later, you choose to. Stoorie never does it on its own.

Will my stories be there if I get a new phone or sign in on a computer?

Your stories and CV lines are saved to your account, so they appear on any device you sign in to. The one thing kept only on your phone is the original, unedited wording with real names in it, because that never leaves your device for your privacy. Carrying that across devices securely is something we are adding.

What is a Spark?

A Spark is a story that is missing one piece, such as what you did, what happened after, or a little context. Stoorie shapes what it can and asks one question to finish it. It never makes up the missing part.

Why did Stoorie ask me to confirm something I pasted?

If pasted text reads as already polished, Stoorie asks you to confirm it is your own and happened as written before saving. This keeps your record honest and yours.

Can I edit or delete my stories?

Yes. You can delete entries, and "Delete all data" in Settings removes everything from our database and clears the original words held on your phone. Deleting cannot be undone.

Who can see my stories?

Only you. Your entries are tied to your account and are not shared with anyone. We do not sell data and we do not show ads.

How much does it cost?

Stoorie is free to start. Some features may become part of a paid plan later, but capturing and keeping your stories is the heart of the product.

How do I get help or send feedback?

Email us any time at admin@stoorie.com, or use Send feedback in Settings. We read every message.
